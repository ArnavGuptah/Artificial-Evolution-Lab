- Dense population centers

## Interpretation

This experiment produced a surprisingly successful ecosystem despite reduced food availability.

The ecosystem discovered stable survival strategies.

Population stability was significantly higher than expected.

---

## Retrospective Notes

Experiment 4 suggests that moderate resource limitation may actually encourage ecosystem stability rather than destabilize it.

This was one of the most surprising results observed.
