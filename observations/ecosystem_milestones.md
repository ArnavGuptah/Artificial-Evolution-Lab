# Ecosystem Milestones

## Milestone 1
Territorial clustering emerged without explicit territorial programming.

## Milestone 2
Edge-specialized organisms appeared.

## Milestone 3
Food scarcity selected slower organisms.

## Milestone 4
Boom-bust cycles emerged naturally.

## Milestone 5
Long-term coexistence of multiple lineages observed.

## Milestone 6
Population stabilization emerged despite ongoing mutation.

## Milestone 7
Green lineage demonstrated repeated resilience across experiments.
