# Hypothesis

Increasing world size will provide more space for organisms to spread.

Expected possibilities:

- Larger and more isolated colonies may form.
- Territorial clustering may become stronger.
- Population may distribute across multiple regions instead of a few dense clusters.
- Species diversity may increase due to reduced competition.
- Migration and colony expansion events may become easier to observe.

The experiment will test whether territorial behavior remains an emergent property in a significantly larger environment.
